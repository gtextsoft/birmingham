The Invitation

This is not a conference. There are no rows of seats and no exhibition hall — only a room, and the right people in it.

The Birmingham Meet & Greet exists for the conversations that are difficult to start over email and impossible to fake at scale: the introductions, the candid advice, and the partnerships that quietly move a business forward.

Why this room

An evening engineered for connection

Every detail is chosen to make the introductions worth making — and worth keeping.

A vetted room

Attendance is reviewed. You spend the evening among operators at your level — not a mailing list.

Candid by design

Off-the-record conversation on capital, scaling and building across the UK–Africa corridor.

Warm introductions

The evening is hosted, not left to chance. The people worth meeting are brought to you.
